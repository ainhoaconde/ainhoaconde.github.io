Step 1

Prepare all ingredients

Step 2

Mix flour, salt and sugar and put everything in a bowl.

Step 3

Cut the butter into small cubes and add to the bowl

Step 4

Cut the butter into small cubes and add to the bowl

Step 5

Knead all the ingredients into a dough until the dough is soft or when you cut the dough with a knife small air bubbles are visible.

Step 6

Let the dough rise in the bowl under a cloth at room temperature until it has doubled in size. Do not knead the dough once it has risen.

Step 7

Divide the dough in the amount of grittibänz and shape the body in parallel preheat the open on 200°C top/bottom heat

Step 8

For the arms and legs you can cut into the body with scissors and shape them then

Step 9

when the open is ready heated you can put the Grittibänz for 10 minutes in the open to bake

Step 10

enjoy your homemade Grittibänz
